Prompts Used

Prompt 1: SCD2 Change Explanation

Analyze the changes between source and target customer datasets.

Tasks:

1. Identify new customer records.
2. Identify modified customer records.
3. Explain the changes in simple business language.
4. Mention whether historical records were preserved.

Output Format:

- Summary
- Changes Detected
- Historical Record Status

---

Prompt 2: Customer Change Summary

Given a list of customer changes, generate a concise summary.

Example Output:

- Customer 101 city changed from Hyderabad to Bangalore.
- Customer 103 was added as a new customer.
- Historical records preserved successfully.

---

AI Capability Demonstrated

The application uses AI-assisted explanation generation to convert technical SCD Type 2 changes into human-readable business summaries.
